Tourist Visa

One of the most popular Temporary Tourist Visas in Canada is the Tourist or visitor visa. This visa is for people who want to go to Canada for a short period of time (usually 6 months or less).

With a tourist visa, you can go to Canada for tourism, holidays, or to visit family. As long as you have all the necessary documents and a clean record, you shouldn't have a problem with Border Patrol Officers at a Canadian port.

There are requirements you must meet if you are to qualify for a tourist visa. They include:

a. A valid legal document like a passport


b. A clean criminal record


c. Proof that you have enough money for the period of your stay


d. Proof that you won't stay beyond the stipulated time


e. A good health record


In addition to that, you may need certain documents like a proof of connection to your country, and an invitation letter from a citizen or permanent resident.

How To Apply for a Tourist Visa
The process of applying for a tourist visa isn't complicated. But you will need to take many steps and fill out several forms. Here are the steps you will need to take:

step 1: make sure you're eligible for a Canadian tourist visa


step 2: fill out all the required documents


step 3: submit your application form


step 4: wait for approval and request for additional information


step 5: send your passport for stamping
